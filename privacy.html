Privacy Policy

What Information I Collect

To provide this service, I collect only what's necessary:

Contact and scheduling: Your name and preferred contact information (email, phone number). Scheduling preferences and session history, managed through an online calendar and scheduling tool.

Work-related: Brief notes from our sessions for my own reference and continuity. Any written homework or exercises you submit.

Payment: Payment information necessary to process cycle payments, handled through a third-party payment processor. I don't store payment details directly.

I don't collect or store more than what's needed for the work.

How I Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

  • Provide the support we've agreed to
  • Maintain continuity between sessions
  • Process payment
  • Contact you about scheduling or logistics

I don't use your information for marketing, research, or any purpose beyond our working relationship.

How Your Information Is Stored

Session notes: Stored securely in physical or password-protected digital files, accessible only to me.

Contact and scheduling information: Managed through an online calendar and scheduling tool.

Payment information: Processed through a third-party payment processor. I don't store payment details directly.

Written submissions (homework, exercises): Stored alongside session notes in the same secure files.

Who Has Access

Only me. I don't share your information with anyone, with the following exceptions:

Safety concerns: If I believe you or someone else is in immediate danger, or if abuse or neglect of a child, elderly person, or dependent adult is disclosed, I'll involve appropriate professionals or authorities as needed.

Testimonials: With your explicit permission only, and anonymized unless you prefer otherwise. You can withdraw this permission at any time.

How Long I Keep Your Information

Information is maintained for the duration of our work together, plus up to six months after our last interaction. After that, it is deleted unless you've requested earlier deletion.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Request a copy of any information I hold about you
  • Request deletion of your notes or contact information
  • Correct any information that's inaccurate

To exercise any of these, just let me know directly.

Website

I don't intentionally track visitors or collect personal information through this website. Any data visible to me is limited to basic site-level information provided by the hosting platform.

Changes to This Policy

If I change how I handle your information, I'll let you know directly before the change takes effect. This policy was last updated [date].

Questions?

If you have questions about how your information is handled, reach out directly. I'll respond within 24-48 hours.
