Ongoing Accompaniment

Ongoing Accompaniment — Quoted per cycle

Work happens in renewable cycles with adaptive involvement. We start with a best guess about what will help, try it, and adjust as we go.

Before beginning ongoing work, we'll have a Groundwork session, a 90-minute conversation to explore your situation, set expectations, and determine how to structure the first cycle. The time for this session is included in your first cycle quote.

How pricing is determined

Ongoing accompaniment is quoted per cycle based on the level of involvement that would actually help right now, and the length of the cycle (often monthly, sometimes shorter early on).

I'll propose a clear structure and price before each cycle begins. Nothing renews automatically, as each cycle will be unique to where you are with your situation.

If at any point the level of involvement feels like too much or not enough, we adjust.

If you want to understand how pricing works before scheduling a Fit Check, reach out directly.

Payment

Payment is due at the start of each cycle for the agreed services. Sessions can be rescheduled with 24+ hours notice. A cancellation policy applies to protect both our time and commitment.

After Work Ends

Safety Line — $30

After our work together ends, you can pay for the option to reach out once during the month, for reassurance, a quick question, or brief recalibration. You might not need it, but it's there if you do.

Available only to those who have completed ongoing accompaniment work. This is not restarting the relationship. It's a light touchpoint while you step fully into independence. This is priced lower than other services partly as thanks for working with me and trusting the process.
