What I Provide

I provide professional accompaniment, short-term, adaptive support while you rebuild clarity, momentum, and trust in your own judgment. This is not therapy, coaching, or consulting, and does not constitute medical, psychological, or legal advice. The specific structure and level of involvement for each engagement is agreed upon before work begins and documented in a cycle summary.

What I Don't Provide

  • Clinical treatment, diagnosis, or mental health care
  • Crisis intervention or emergency support
  • Guarantees about outcomes
  • Advice that substitutes for professional medical, legal, or financial guidance

If you need any of the above, I'd encourage you to seek support from a trained professional. See Scope & Boundaries for full details.

Your Responsibilities

  • Show up to scheduled sessions or cancel with appropriate notice (see Cancellation Policy)

My Responsibilities

Payment

Payment is due at the start of each cycle for the agreed services. Payment terms, refunds, and cancellation details are outlined in the Cancellation Policy.

Ending the Work

We make space for at least one conversation about why, unless circumstances make that impossible. If I end the work, I'll explain why directly. If you end the work, I'll respect that without pressure.

No Guarantee of Availability

I work with very few people at a time. Availability is not guaranteed beyond what we've agreed to for a given cycle. I don't provide after-hours, on-call, or crisis support.

Disagreements

If a disagreement arises, we'll work to resolve it directly. If we can't reach agreement, either party is free to end the working relationship. Refund and cancellation terms are outlined in the Cancellation Policy.
